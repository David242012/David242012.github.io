Simple Gambling Game

Welcome to the official page for the Simple Gambling Game Chrome Extension!

In this game, you can place bets and roll a dice to test your luck. It's a fun and straightforward way to simulate gambling without any real money involved. All transactions use virtual currency, ensuring a safe and entertaining experience for all users.

Features

  • Bet virtual coins
  • Roll a dice and win or lose
  • No real money involved
  • Simple, clean, and fun gameplay
